General Eligibility

These are common criteria. Exact requirements can vary by batch, trade, and center.

Citizenship & ID

Pakistani citizen with a valid CNIC (or B-Form for some youth programs). Domicile may be required for specific projects.

Age

Typically 18‚Äì30. Some vocational trades allow up to 40. Age relaxations depend on program guidelines.

Education

Matric/Intermediate for most skills. Advanced IT (AI/DS/Web) may require a Bachelor‚Äôs in a relevant field.

Experience

Not mandatory for basic trades. Prior NAVTTC trainees can be ineligible for some intakes.

Availability

Commit to full-time classes (3‚Äì6 months typical). Minimum attendance rules apply for certification.

Assessments

Entry test and/or interview may apply. Final certification requires passing internal and competency assessments.

How to Apply (NSIS Portal)

1
Create Account

Go to the official NSIS portal, sign up with your CNIC & mobile, complete OTP verification, and set a password.

2
Fill Profile

Enter personal details, education, address, and (if any) employment info. Upload clear photos & documents.

3
Choose Course & Center

Select your trade, preferred training provider/center, and shift. Review eligibility shown on the listing.

4
Submit & Track

Submit the application. For paid intakes, generate a challan and add payment details. Track status on your dashboard.

5
Assessment & Joining

Download roll-number slip if there‚Äôs a test. On selection, visit the center with originals for verification & orientation.
